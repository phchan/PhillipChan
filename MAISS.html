Management Information Student Society's mission revolves around strengthening the business and technology community at UC Irvine. MAISS is an organization with a tight-knit community of students who focus on building themselves professionally. MAISS offers business and tech related workshops such as intro to SQL/Business Analytics, website/portfolio building for job applications, and company tours at Google, Blizzard, and others.
